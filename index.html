Research Overview

My research interests broadly include topics in learning, optimization, and control of network systems, with applications to cyber-physical systems, complex industrial processes, and wireless sensor networks. I am currently a senior member of the Advanced Alarm Management and Design (AAMD) research group at the University of Alberta. Our research activities are funded by an NSERC (Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council) Alliance Grant with industrial partners Syncrude Canada and Enbridge Inc. My recent research includes:

  • Learning and optimization-based control system design,

  • Anomaly detection and analysis in cyber-physical systems,

  • Co-design of communication networks and control systems,

  • Consensus control and topology design of multi-agent systems.
